Privacy Policy — Realtor Finder

Effective date: 9 July 2026 · Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Realtor Finder ("the app", "we") handles information. In short: the app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It runs entirely on your device.

1. Information we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no login, and no sign-up. We do not collect, record, or transmit your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, device identifiers, or usage analytics. The app keeps no database and stores no search history — results exist only in the app's memory while it is open and are gone when you leave the screen.

2. How the app works

When you run a search, the app sends a standard web request from your device to publicly available real-estate listing websites in the market you select, and displays the results (including the estate agent contact details that those sites publish publicly). These requests contain no personal information about you — they are the same kind of request your web browser makes when visiting those sites. The retrieved listings and contact details are shown to you and are not saved.

3. Contacting an agent (hand-off)

When you choose to contact an estate agent, the app simply opens the relevant app already installed on your device — for example WhatsApp, your default SMS/messaging app, or your phone dialer — with the agent's public number filled in. You write and send any message yourself. The app never sends messages on your behalf, and it does not access your contacts or store the numbers. Once you are handed off to those third-party apps, their own privacy policies apply (for example, WhatsApp).

4. Data sharing and selling

Because we collect no data, we have nothing to share or sell. We do not use advertising networks, tracking technologies, cookies, or third-party analytics SDKs.

5. Data retention and deletion

The app stores no personal data, so there is nothing to retain or delete. Uninstalling the app removes it entirely from your device.

6. Permissions

The app requests only Internet access, used solely to fetch public listings. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or storage.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at this URL.
